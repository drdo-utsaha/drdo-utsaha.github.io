The Sanskrit word 'TURIYA' means the fourth dimension of human consciousness. The Vedas, considered as the crest jewel of all knowledge, describe 'TURIYA' as the highest state of human consciousness transcending the lower physical, mental and the intellectual platform. The three other states are Jaagrta (wakefulness), Swapna (Dream state) and Sushupta (deep sleep).

Bhakti Vinoda Swami

Bhakti Vinoda Swami is a world renowned spiritual coach committed to help people to orient their lives into a genuine spiritual life.

An engineer from Regional engineering college (now NIT) Trichy and a project manager from Grindwell Norton & Co, Bangalore, he has pioneered various R&D projects in India and abroad.

Attracted to the teachings of Bhagavad Gita, he has dedicated his life for the education and practice of the teachings and has taken it up as a lifetime career.

Bhakti Vinoda swami is the founder of TURIYA Academia and is involved in many educational and cultural initiatives with the Government of India

He holds the position of Director/Chairman of several educational trusts, philanthropic and humanitarian institutions. He travels world wide conducting various leadership summits, workshops, seminars and courses in corporate organizations and for youth in prestigious educational institutions.

As a passionate visionary, Bhakti Vinoda Swami continues to be an empowering guide to whoever seeking a higher thinking paradigm.
